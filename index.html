George Weykamp is an aspiring legal and regulatory journalist at the University of Michigan. He has interned on the news desks at Michigan Radio, Automotive News, and Bloomberg Law where he has covered a variety of beats — ranging from the Automotive Industry to the U.S Equal Employment Opportunity Commission.

He was the 2022 University Editor at The Michigan Daily where he oversaw coverage of the first firing of a University President in over a century and a historic sexual misconduct settlement.

George's work has appeared in the Politico Playbook, Bloomberg TV, National Public Radio, SCOTUS Blog, Above the Law and has been cited by the U.S Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit.
